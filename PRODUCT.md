# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Individuals and trusted family organizers who need quick, dependable access to identity, immigration, insurance, vision, and medical records—often from a phone while filling out a form, traveling, or helping someone else.

## Product Purpose

Sleeve is one private place for the details and source documents that are otherwise scattered across drawers, inboxes, and portals. It should make a record easy to find, understand, renew, and selectively share without making sensitive information feel casual.

## Brand Personality

Warm, discreet, and exact. The product should feel reassuring without becoming sentimental, and highly secure without borrowing the fear-driven visual language of cybersecurity products.

## Anti-references

Avoid the stale density of hospital portals, the cold blue-and-gray visual shorthand of insurance software, fake security theater, fintech-vault theatrics, glassmorphism, and repetitive icon-card dashboards.

## Design Principles

1. Reveal only what the moment needs; conceal sensitive values by default.
2. Make the next action obvious when a record is expiring, incomplete, or ready to share.
3. Treat every person as a distinct private workspace with clear ownership and access boundaries.
4. Let warmth come from language, care, and a restrained living color—not from ornamental surfaces.
5. Make security legible through specific behaviors: short-lived links, audit history, encryption, and explicit recipient controls.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard and screen-reader navigation, high-contrast text, clear focus states, touch targets of at least 44px, reduced motion, error messages that do not rely on color, and mobile layouts down to 320px.
